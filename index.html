About

Biplab Sarkar is a passionate PhD Scholar and Teaching Assistant at the Department of Energy Science and Engineering, IIT Bombay, India. He holds a Master of Science in Physics with a specialization in Electronics from Visva-Bharati University, Santiniketan. His research focuses on memristive systems and neuromorphic computing, with broader interests spanning Electronics, Semiconductor Physics, Instrumentation, Experimental Condensed Matter Physics, Nano-Fabrication, and Quantum-dot Cellular Automata.

Driven by a commitment to advancing the frontiers of Physics and interdisciplinary innovation, he strives to make meaningful contributions through memristor-based modeling, nonlinear dynamics, and hardware-oriented neural computation. As a strong advocate of teamwork and collaborative learning, he has actively mentored and advised science clubs, combining his technical expertise with a deep appreciation of socio-cultural dynamics.
